## Cabbage Casserole
Serving Size: 6
Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Categories: Casseroles Vegetables

8   cups cabbage, thinly sliced
1   can celery soup
1/2 cup salad dressing (ie: Miracle Whip)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
2   cups corn flakes
2   tablespoon butter, melted
1/2 cup cheese, shredded
