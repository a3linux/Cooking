## Pudding Cake

* **Serving Size:** 1
* **Categories:** Desserts

```
 1      stick margarine

 2      cups sugar

 1/2    cup cocoa

 1/2    cups milk

 1      teaspoon vanilla

 1/2    cup peanut butter

 3 1/2  cup oatmeal
```

Boil first four ingredients for two minutes.

Add vanilla, peanut butter and oatmeal. Stir until mixed,

Drop using a teaspoon on wax paper. Let cool.
